# Automate Your Customer Engagement: Introducing the n8n Braze Community Node

We're excited to announce that Velocity BPA has released a new community node for n8n that brings the power of Braze directly into your automation workflows!

## The Challenge: Disconnected Marketing Tools

Marketing teams today juggle multiple platforms to manage customer engagement. Braze is a powerful customer engagement platform that handles multi-channel messaging, campaign management, and user segmentation. However, connecting Braze with your other business tools often requires custom development or expensive integration platforms.

That's where our new n8n-nodes-braze comes in.

## What Can You Do With This Node?

The n8n Braze node unlocks powerful automation capabilities for your marketing and customer engagement workflows:

- **User Management**: Automatically sync user data between your CRM, database, or other systems and Braze. Create, update, or delete user profiles based on triggers from anywhere in your stack.

- **Campaign Automation**: Trigger Braze campaigns based on events in other systems. Completed a purchase? Trigger a thank-you campaign. Abandoned cart? Start a re-engagement sequence.

- **Multi-Channel Messaging**: Coordinate email, push notifications, SMS, and in-app messages across your entire customer journey, triggered by real-time events.

- **Advanced Segmentation**: Dynamically manage user segments based on data from multiple sources, ensuring your messaging always reaches the right audience.

## Real-World Use Cases

Imagine automatically adding new Stripe customers to specific Braze segments, triggering onboarding campaigns when users complete certain actions in your app, or syncing customer support tickets to trigger targeted messaging campaigns. The possibilities are endless.

## Getting Started

Installing the Braze node is simple. In your n8n instance, run:


npm install n8n-nodes-braze


Then restart n8n, and you'll find the Braze node available in your node palette.

For detailed documentation, examples, and to contribute, visit our GitHub repository: https://github.com/Velocity-BPA/n8n-nodes-braze
